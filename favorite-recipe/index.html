Mozzarella Sticks

A very yummy deep fried stick of mozzarella cheese, included with marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella Stick Ingredients
    • 16 oz mozzarella string cheese
    • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • large eggs
    • Tbsp whole milk, or can substitute water (milk prefererred)
    • 1 1/2 cups Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs or Panko
    • /2 Tbsp garlic powder
    • vegetable, canola, or peanut oil for frying process
  • Marinara Sauce Ingredients
    • 1 can (28 oz) of tomatos
    • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
    • 4-7 minced cloves of garlic
    • 1 large fresh basil sprig (or 1 tsp dried oregano) and a pinch of red pepper flakes
    • 1/2 yellow onion, finely-diced
    • 1 Tsp salt
    • 1 Tsp sugar (optional)

Directions for Mozzarella Sticks

  1. Cut mozzarella stick in half.
  2. Breading Station-- Use three shallow bowls:
    1. Bowl 1: Flour mixd with garlic powder.
    2. Bowl 2: Eggs beaten with choosing of whole or water.
    3. Bowl 3: Breadcrumbs
  3. Bread the sticks:
    1. Coat cheese sticks in flour mixture, shaking off excess.
    2. Dip in egg mixture.
    3. Coat with breadcrumbs, pressing gently to help the crumbs stick.
    4. Repeat the egg and breadcrumb steps, to make a "double-coating", to ensuring no leakage or bursting.
  4. Freeze: Place breadsticks onto a parchment-lined baking sheet for at least 1 hour. (8-24 hrs for best result)
  5. Fry: Heat 2-3 inches of oil in a bottom-heavy pot to 360-365 Fahrenheit.
  6. Cook: Fry in small portions (3-4 at a time) for 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Drain and serve: Remove sticks from oil with a slotted spoon onto a paper towel-lined plate. Serve immidiately with warm marinara sauce.

Directions for Marinara Sauce

  1. Saute: Heat pan to medium heat, saute the diced onion until translucent, then add minced garlic for one minute until fragrant, not browned.
  2. Simmer: Stir in the crushed tomatos, salt, and spices. Simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens and when the surface oil turns a deep orange.
  3. Finishing touch: Add basil leaves to the mixture right before serving to keep the flavor bright.
