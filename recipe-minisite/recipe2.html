Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy chocolate chip cookies with crisp edges and melty chocolate throughout.

Ingredients

  • Cookie Dough Ingredients
    • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • ½ tsp salt
    • ¾ cup unsalted butter, melted
    • ½ cup granulated sugar
    • 1 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • Optional Add ins
    • ½ cup chopped walnuts
    • ½ cup white chocolate chips
    • Pinch of cinnamon
    • Flaky sea salt for topping

Directions for Cookies

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Prepare dry ingredients:
    1. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
    2. Set aside.
  3. Mix wet ingredients:
    1. Combine melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
    2. Beat in egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract.
    3. Stir in dry ingredients until just combined.
    4. Fold in chocolate chips and optional add ins.
  4. Chill dough for 20–30 minutes for thicker cookies.
  5. Portion: Scoop 1–2 tbsp of dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Bake: Cook for 10–12 minutes until edges are lightly golden.
  7. Cool and serve: Let cookies rest on the sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a rack.

Directions for Optional Toppings

  1. Sea Salt: Sprinkle a small pinch on each cookie right after baking.
  2. Extra Chocolate: Press a few chocolate chips into the tops while warm.
  3. Walnut Finish: Add chopped nuts before baking for a crunchy texture.
