Chicken Alfredo Pasta

A creamy, comforting pasta dish made with tender chicken, rich Alfredo sauce, and perfectly cooked fettuccine. Simple to prepare and always a crowd‑pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Alfredo Ingredients
    • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
    • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
    • 2 Tbsp olive oil
    • 2 Tbsp butter
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
    • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Optional Add‑Ins
    • ½ cup steamed broccoli
    • ½ cup sautéed mushrooms
    • Red pepper flakes for heat
    • Extra Parmesan for serving

Directions for Chicken Alfredo

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare chicken:
    1. Season sliced chicken with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    3. Cook chicken until golden and fully cooked; remove and set aside.
  3. Make the Alfredo sauce:
    1. Melt butter in the same skillet.
    2. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Pour in heavy cream and simmer for 3–4 minutes.
    4. Whisk in Parmesan until smooth and creamy.
  4. Combine: Add cooked chicken and pasta into the sauce, tossing to coat.
  5. Simmer for 1–2 minutes to thicken slightly.
  6. Serve: Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan if desired.
  7. Enjoy warm for best texture and flavor.

Directions for Optional Add‑Ins

  1. Broccoli: Steam lightly and fold into the pasta before serving.
  2. Mushrooms: Sauté in butter until browned, then add to the sauce.
  3. Heat: Sprinkle red pepper flakes on top for a spicy kick.
